History Of Construction

The History of construction is fascinating and takes thousands of years. The construction industry has grown time by time. Earlier, people used to shelter in caves and wooden homes. There were no technologies, and knowledge of construction existed. People get construction knowledge time by time. As time grew, new technologies, materials, and methods emerged.
